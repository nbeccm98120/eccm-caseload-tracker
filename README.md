# SC Caseload Tracker

Desktop application for Support Coordinators managing ODP caseloads.

---

## What It Does

A Windows desktop app that centralizes all SC tracking requirements in one place. Data is stored locally and backed up automatically to a designated folder every 7 minutes and on close.

---

## Pages & Features

**Dashboard**  
At-a-glance alerts for anything overdue, due this month, or due next month across all categories. Alerts can be filtered, dismissed, and restored.

**Caseload**  
Full consumer list with Active/Inactive/All status, search, column filters, and sorting. Add, edit, and manage consumers. Export records for case transfers.

**Monitoring**  
Track monthly and quarterly monitoring contacts per waiver frequency. Logs contact type, date, and tool completion date. Status tracking: Done, Pending, Critical, Missed, Not Due.

**ISP Tracker**  
Full ISP deadline pipeline calculated automatically from the Annual Review date — Start, Last ISP, Invite Due, Submit By, and Future ARUD. Tracks status, variance, and submitted date.

**PUNS**  
Tracks finalized date, auto-calculates due date, and logs submission to coach.

**Emergency / FFA / Voter**  
Annual renewal tracking for Emergency Preparedness, FFA (Free from Abuse), and Voter Registration. Each renews independently. Voter registration is age-gated for consumers under 18.

**A/B Tracker**  
Monthly A and B code logging with counters. Tracks Met, In Progress, Not Started, and Missed statuses.

**Export / Import**  
Export selected consumers for backup or case transfers. Import records from other SCs with full conflict detection, preview diffs, and merge/skip options.
